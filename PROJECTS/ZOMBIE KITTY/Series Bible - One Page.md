## Joe
- Series protagonist
- Bit by the zombie kitty and infected, slowly becoming a full on zombie
- Nerdy-ish
- Loves zombies
- Plays minecraft
- Slightly socially awkward
- Very much wants to 'fit in' and 'be cool'
- Doesn't like to ask for help
- Hides problems from parents

## Zombie Kitty
- Milo
- Calico
- Turned to a zombie after eating infected mice created by his owner
- More intelligent that in it seems
- Understands that biting leads to infection

## Elma Davis (Mad Scientist)
- Invented a cereal *Crunchy-O's* that never gets soggy in milk
- Owner of Otis (a chihuahua) and Milo (a calico), named after the animals in Milo and Otis
- When Otis dies of old age she retires and goes into seclusion, searching for a way to extend the lives of animals
- She infected some mice with her research, which Milo ate, eventually leading to his zombification
- She eventually finds out that Joe has been infected and wants to research him as well
- Initial antagonist, but becomes an ally when the a gov't shadow agency moves in

## Zombification Rules
- Only spread by saliva into open wounds
- Virus dies outside of host
- Progression is slower the larger the host
- The destruction of the brain is the only way to kill a zombie
- Symptoms
	- Heart rate slows until it eventually stops
	- Respiration no longer needed
	- No healing factor
	- Body does not decay
	- Body begins to smell
	- Sleep is unneeded
	- No more growth
	- Cannot get tired (can run/walk basically forever)
	- Desire to infect others (manifested by the desire to eat flesh)
